# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to `Defender` and its related game modes.

## Information We Collect

`Defender` is designed to collect as little information as possible.

The game does not intentionally collect:

- personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or home address
- audio recordings or microphone input
- precise location information
- contacts, photos, or messages

The game may process limited device and app information required for normal operation, such as:

- device type and operating system
- app version
- basic diagnostic information generated by the platform or app store

## How Information Is Used

Any limited technical information is used only to:

- run the game correctly on your device
- play audio and visual effects
- support updates, stability, and bug fixes

## Audio and Microphone

`Defender` uses audio playback for music and sound effects.

`Defender` does not record audio and does not use your microphone.

## Data Sharing

`Defender` does not sell your personal information.

`Defender` does not share personal information with third parties except where required for core platform services, such as:

- Google Play or Apple App Store distribution
- crash reporting, diagnostics, or system-level services provided by the operating system or platform

## Data Storage

Gameplay settings and progress may be stored locally on your device. This data is used only to support the game experience.

## Children

`Defender` is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by revising the date at the top of this page.
